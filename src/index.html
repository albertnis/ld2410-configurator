LD2410 Configurator

Serial

Connect your LD2410 to a serial-to-USB adapter:

  • Connect 5V to VCC and GND to GND
  • Connect RX to TX and TX to RX
  • Ensure adapter is connected to this device with any necessary drivers installed

Bluetooth

Connect your LD2410 to Bluetooth:

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on the LD2410
  • Connect 5V to VCC and GND to GND
